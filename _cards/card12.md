Assembled a Stomp Sheild created by [Open Music Labs](http://www.openmusiclabs.com/projects/stomp-shield/index.html). I decided to make a chiptune style arpegiator for this pedal. The process 
involved modifying the up down sketch to alternate up and normal. This is the most useful arpeggio when soloing over 
chiptune.

- [github repo](https://github.com/joeymariano/stomp-arp)
