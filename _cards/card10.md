Assembled devices for the [Queen Mary Escape Room](https://queenmaryescape.com) in L.A. with [Knabe Labs](https://knabelabs.com/). This device uses an [ESP32](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ESP32) to control an IR sensor, LEDs, a button, a switch and an OLED screen.
